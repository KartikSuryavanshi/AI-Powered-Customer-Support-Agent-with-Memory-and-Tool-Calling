# Billing FAQ

If a customer reports duplicate charges:

1. Verify transaction IDs in billing service.
2. Confirm if one charge is an authorization hold.
3. If duplicate is confirmed, submit refund ticket and share expected refund timeline (5-7 business days).
4. Mark billing status as under review.

For overdue accounts:

- Share invoice details.
- Offer invoice PDF and payment link.
- Confirm service restrictions if account is 30+ days overdue.
